Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NIFAR ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website, digital products, and related services.

1. Who We Are

NIFAR is a digital education and digital products brand. We sell guides, cheat sheets, blueprint packs, and related digital content, primarily through our storefront on Whop.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Purchase information: When you buy a product from us through Whop, Whop processes your name, email address, and payment details on our behalf. We receive limited order information (such as your email and product purchased) needed to deliver your product and provide support.
  • Support communications: If you email us or message us for support, we retain that correspondence to help resolve your query.
  • Website/analytics data: Our pages may use basic analytics (such as page views) to understand how visitors use our content. This is aggregated and not used to individually identify you.

3. Social Media & Publishing Platforms

NIFAR uses automation tools to publish content (videos, images, and posts) to our own official accounts on platforms including Pinterest, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and Reddit. These integrations are used solely to publish content from NIFAR to NIFAR's own accounts. We do not use these platform APIs to collect, access, or store personal data belonging to visitors, followers, or other third-party users of those platforms.

4. How We Use Information

  • To deliver purchased digital products and provide customer support
  • To respond to inquiries
  • To improve our products, content, and marketing
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate our business (such as Whop for payment processing, and cloud/automation providers used to deliver content), solely to the extent needed to provide our services.

6. Data Retention

We retain order and support information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our products and services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
